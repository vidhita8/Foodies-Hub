Alfredo Pasta

Creamy, silky, cheesy Alfredo pasta, marvelously garlicky with twirly pasta...

INGREDIENTS:

  • Pasta-Fettuccine Pasta.
  • Butter-Alfredo sauce starts with butter.
  • Cream-Rich sauce requires almost heavy cream.
  • Seasoning-Salt, pepper, garlic salt.
  • Cheese-Romano and parmesan cheese.

DIRECTIONS:

  1. Cook pasta accordingly.
  2. Warm butter and cream in a saucepan. Season it with salt and freshly ground black pepper.
  3. Grate the parseman cheese and place half of it into a serving bowl. Pour warm buttet/cream over the top.
  4. Now pour pasta immediately into bowl. Toss it couple of times, then turn it into other half of the parseman. Toss to combine, thinning with pasta water.
  5. Serve it hot!!

Pizza

Light, crispy pizza with superfascinating toppings

Ingredients:

  • Yeast- As leavening agent.
  • Sugar- To activate yeast.
  • Olive oil- For barrier between oil and water that prevents sogginess.
  • Cheese- Mozzarella cheese for stringyness.
  • Herbs and Spices- Oregano, basil, garlic powder and other as per your need.

Directions:

  1. Knead the dough.
  2. Make pizza sauce with tomatoes, garlic and olive oil, let them cook for 20 min until tomatoes softened. Then add salt and all herbs and crushed black pepper. Mix it well and set aside.
  3. Preheat oven and grease the base with olive oil.
  4. Now, take dough and roll it with a rolling pin. After that prick the dough with fork for thinner and crisper crust.
  5. Brush the crust with olive oil and spread tomato sauce on it. Top it with mozzrella cheese and spread veggies.
  6. Add a light layer of parmesan cheese and bake it for 10-15 min on lower heat until cheese melts.
  7. Serve it hot with chilli flakes and oregano!!!

Wai Wai

Easy to cook. Devouring hot snack with harmonious blend of savoury, spicy and aromatic elements.

Ingredients:

  • Noodles
  • Vegies-Tofu, cabbage, capsicum, onion, green onion
  • Sauce-Tomato ketchup, soya sauce, green chilli
  • Seasoning-Salt, black pepper
  • Oil-Olive oil or any vegetable oil.

Directions:

  1. Take out noodles from packet. Add two cups of warm water to a bowl to soften them.
  2. Let the noodles soak for 10 minutes until soft. Using a strainer, strain noodles once they are soft.
  3. Pour oil into the pan. Once the oil is hot, add onion. Fry it for 2-3 min on medium-high heat until the onion softens and turns light brown.
  4. Add vegies after that, mix and let it fry for 3-4 min.
  5. Add soaked noodles and mix it well.
  6. Add seasonings and sauce to it.
